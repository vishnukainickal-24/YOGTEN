OUR STORY

From Kerala Coconuts to Your Home

At YOGTEN, we honor the age-old practices of Kerala's coconut artisans. Every batch of our Virgin Coconut Oil is crafted with fresh coconut milk, slow-heated in traditional clay pots to preserve natural flavor, nutrients, and aroma.

We believe in honest ingredients, sustainable farming, and a wellness journey grounded in purity. Our mission is to bring you a product that nourishes skin, strengthens hair, and supports holistic health every day.

  • ✔ Made from freshly harvested coconuts
  • ✔ Prepared using traditional hot process
  • ✔ Free from chemicals and refinements
  • ✔ Produced locally in Kottayam, Kerala
